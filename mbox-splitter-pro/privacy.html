Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mbox Splitter Pro ("the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Summary: Mbox Splitter Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or third parties. All data remains on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any information. The App operates entirely on your local device without sending any data to our servers.

How the App Works

Mbox Splitter Pro performs the following operations locally on your Mac:

  • File Reading: When you select an MBOX file, the App reads it from your local filesystem to analyze its contents and structure.
  • File Splitting: The App splits large MBOX archives into smaller, manageable files based on your chosen criteria (by size, by date, or by message count). All processing happens locally on your device.
  • File Writing: Split MBOX files are saved to a location you choose on your Mac.

The App does not connect to any external servers during this process.

Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • App Sandbox: The App runs in Apple's App Sandbox, limiting its access to only files you explicitly grant permission to.
  • No Network Access: The App does not require or use any network connections.
  • No External Services: The App does not connect to any servers whatsoever.

Third-Party Services

The App does not communicate with any third-party services. We do not use any analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or other third-party services.

Data We Do NOT Collect

To be explicit, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Usage statistics or patterns
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Your email content, metadata, or attachments
  • Analytics or telemetry data

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All files are processed and stored locally on your device, and you have full control over them.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
