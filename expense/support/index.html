Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my budget?

When you first open eXpense, the Setup flow guides you through choosing a budget period and amount. You can change these anytime in Settings.

How do I add an expense?

On the main screen, type the amount using the calculator keypad and tap the checkmark button to save.

Can I change the currency?

Yes. Go to Settings and select a different currency. Note: changing currency will reset your expense history to avoid mixing different currencies.

Where is my data stored?

All your expense data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not have servers that receive your financial data. Deleting the app removes all data.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Upgrade to Premium". Then tap "Restore Purchases" to restore a previously purchased subscription.

Does eXpense sync between devices?

Currently, data is stored per-device. iCloud sync may be added in a future update.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to manage or cancel.
