Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 6, 2026

Layout Master ("we", "us", or "the app") is an iPhone home screen organization tool for macOS. It helps you reorganize your iPhone apps using productivity methodology presets. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data Controller

The data controller for information processed by Layout Master is:

TechConcepts
Email: privacy@techconcepts.org

2. Data We Collect

Data Purpose Storage
iPhone app list (names only) Categorize and organize apps into layout presets On-device only
Selected layout preset Apply your chosen organizational system (GTD, Deep Work, etc.) On-device only

All data stays on your Mac. Layout Master does not transmit any data over the internet.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

Layout Master does not collect:

  • Personal identity information (name, email, phone number)
  • App usage data or behavior analytics
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • The content of any app on your iPhone (only app names are read)

4. iPhone Connection

Layout Master reads your iPhone's installed app list via a local USB or Wi-Fi connection to your Mac. This data transfer happens entirely between your devices on your local network. No data is sent to any external server.

5. Third-Party Services

Layout Master does not integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics SDKs. The app is entirely self-contained.

6. Data Storage & Deletion

Layout configurations are stored locally on your Mac. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data. You can also clear your layout data at any time from within the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Layout Master does not collect personal data from any user, including children. The app is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
