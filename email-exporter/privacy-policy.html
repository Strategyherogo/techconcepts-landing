üîí Overview

Email Exporter is a Chrome extension that helps users export their emails to PDF format. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

üìä Data Collection

What the extension accesses:

  • Email content: The extension reads email content (subject, body, sender, date, attachments) only when you explicitly click the "Export" button
  • This data stays on your device: All processing happens locally in your browser
  • No data is sent to any server: We have no backend, no analytics, no tracking

What we DON'T do:

  • We do NOT collect personal information
  • We do NOT track your browsing activity
  • We do NOT store your emails anywhere
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT use cookies or analytics

üíæ Data Storage

This extension does NOT use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.
