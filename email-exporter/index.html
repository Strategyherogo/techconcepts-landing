Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work with my email provider?

Yes! Email Exporter works with Gmail (mail.google.com), Outlook (outlook.live.com, outlook.office.com), and Yahoo Mail (mail.yahoo.com).

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. We never see, store, or have access to your emails.

Is it really free?

Yes, completely free. No premium tier, no subscriptions, no hidden limits. Just install and use.

Can I export multiple emails at once?

Yes! The extension exports all messages in an email thread, with an optional summary page showing categories and stats.

What are keyword filters?

Smart filters that categorize emails by content. Select "Financial" to export only emails mentioning invoices, payments, etc.

Does it download attachments?

The PDF includes clickable links to attachments. Click a link to download directly from your email provider.
